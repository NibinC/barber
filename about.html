About Ayman Hair Studio

Two decades of expertise in hairstyling!

Welcome to our Ayman Hair Studio, where we seamlessly blend beauty and expertise in a harmonious mix of creativity and precision. With over two decades of experience, our accomplished team of hairstylists takes pride in delivering unmatched skill and artistry to every client's unique journey. Nestled in the heart of Newmarket, our studio is more than just a space for hair transformations—it's a sanctuary where individuality is celebrated, and dreams come to life. Whether you're seeking a fabulous blow-dry, stunning hair color, highlights, balayage, or a stylish cut for both men and women, we offer a range of services at affordable prices. From hair extensions to indulgent treatments like Brazilian Collagen Hair Smooth Treatment and Hair Botox Smooth Treatment, we've got you covered. Our commitment to excellence is evident in our partnerships with premier beauty brands, including L'Oréal Professional Paris, amika, and Pureology Professional Color Care. Our journey includes shaping the aesthetics of Dubai's media landscape and contributing to the glamour of well-known celebrities. With a foundation in excellence gained at L'Oréal, we've crafted a unique space that mirrors our dedication to innovation and sophistication. Trust us to turn your hair dreams into a stunning reality, whether it's a chic haircut, a vibrant color transformation, or specialized styling for that special occasion.

Join us at Ayman Hair Studio, where each appointment is an opportunity to redefine beauty and embrace the artistry of hairstyling.
